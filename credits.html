What is the XUGS Infopad?

XUGS Infopad is an application built for the benefit of the Xavier University Grade School - Ateneo de Cagayan's pupils, teachers, parents and other community members.

XUGS Infopad is an android application. If your school is interested in creating your own application, you can contact the author for help regarding making your own school application.

This application started as a project of the TechSurfers Club (Computer Club) SY 2013-2014 of XUGS. Since it was unfinished, the author continued its development and testing. This application is still in beta mode. Thus this application still needs hardware testing and feedback from users.
